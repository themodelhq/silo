Every Salary Already Has a Job.

Silo reads your payslip, works out your real net pay, and sorts it into envelopes for rent, transport, savings and more — automatically. No AI, no guesswork: just transparent rules you control.

How it works

1. Paste or scan your payslip

Drop in a PDF, photo, or pasted text. Our deterministic parser extracts every line item using documented rules — never a black box.

2. Money lands in envelopes

Configurable percentage or fixed rules allocate net salary the moment it's calculated — rent, transport, savings, and whatever else you define.

3. Track, adjust, stay in budget

Log expenses against envelopes, see what's left in real time, and get reminded before rent or DSTV is due.
